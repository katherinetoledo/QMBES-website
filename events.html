2020/2021 Events


Due COVID-19, all 2020 events will be organised online in order to maintain our community safe.

This year QMBES is planning weekly workshops, talks and panels with senior executives, inspirational speakers and entrepreneurs in a variety of segments.
In addition to that, QMBES will organise the II QMBES Entrepreneurship Competition in our Campus during the 2nd semester.
