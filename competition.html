II QMBES Entrepreneurship Competition

It is coming back in Jan/2021...


Our competition registration has not begun yet, but all Quen Mary students can join this journey in Jan/2021.
It does not matter if you do not have a business idea, a team or experience; the competition welcomes anyone open to learning and interested in meeting like-minded individuals and getting inspired.

Important points:
  • Several special workshops and events will be delivered to support the participants.
  • If you do not have a team or an idea, do not worry, we will develop it during the competition.
  • The winning team will receive a capital investment prize!


How Does It Work?


Step 1: Join

All Queen Mary Students can join with an already formed team or as an individual looking forward to creating or participating in a group.


Step 2: Attend Workshops

Our workshops will cover different fundamental topics, such as idea formation and validation, funding, financials, team building and pitching. Dates TBC

Important points:
  1. Learn ways and utilise tools to help you come up with business ideas.
  2. Hear and get support from successful entrepreneurs.
  3. Find out how to apply to the competition and what the judges are looking for.
  4. Get feedback and support to develop your idea.
  5. Meet like-minded students and form your ideal team.

Step 3: Compete

Pitch in front of a panel of expert judges from different industry backgrounds.
It is a unique opportunity to get exposure, build your CV with new skills, validate your idea, and win a prize (Â£TBC)!
Final pitching rounds should be held in March. Dates TBC.
