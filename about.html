Our Mission


A society that fosters opportunities for QM students to explore their career options whilst building memorable and long-lasting relations with others. We establish valuable and significant lessons to create a community of innovators and business minded people. We do not only provide guidance and insights into the business world and career perspective, but also together we create a diverse and inclusive environment which builds upon QM students’ initiative to reach their full potential. QMBES is there every step of the way.


Our Values


At QMBES we aim to create a positive impact on Queen Mary's students, and provide a place where our members can develop their fullest & true potential, feel inspired, build their network and take-on challenging opportunities.
